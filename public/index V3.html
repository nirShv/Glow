Doktorabc Glow prototype · Product assessment demo · Not a medical product
This prototype is illustrative and does not represent live clinical guidance, treatment approval or medical advice.
Prototype booking

In the real product, this step would reserve a clinic slot, collect a deposit and share the patient's structured brief with the selected provider.

The provider receives a summary including treatment interest, screening responses, relevant health notes and the patient's stated goals — no raw medical data is shared without consent.

How Glow protects the journey

Doktorabc prepares the patient and provider with structured information, but does not replace clinical judgement. The provider confirms final eligibility, prescription where required, consent and treatment on-site.

Doktorabc owns: patient guidance, pre-screening, booking, payment, records, check-ins and recall.

Clinic / provider owns: final eligibility, prescription where required, informed consent, treatment delivery, product traceability and clinical escalation.

Glow Pass benefits

Glow Pass helps patients plan, finance and maintain treatment safely over time. It supports continuity, follow-up and rebooking without creating pressure to treat.

Included: virtual pre-consults, priority booking, treatment timeline, photo tracking, member pricing and safe recall reminders.

Membership does not guarantee treatment. Every treatment still requires clinical eligibility and provider approval.

Provider application received

In a real product, Doktorabc Ops would review credentials, validate the clinical setup and approve the provider before any patient bookings are activated.

Review time: 2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with next steps, including scheduling a clinic visit during the pilot phase.

Provider requirements

To join Doktorabc Glow, providers must hold:

Valid medical registration (GMC, NMC, or equivalent per country) · Current professional indemnity insurance · Documented aesthetic training · Suitable clinical premises · Authorised product supply path · Compliance with local prescribing regulations

All credentials are verified by the Doktorabc compliance team before any patient bookings are activated.
